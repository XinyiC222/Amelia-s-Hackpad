# Amelia-s-Hackpad

My personal Macropad that would help me open up apps, turn volume up and down, and do other cool things. I am using: 4 SW_Push, and 1 rotary encoder, 2 neopixels, and a Seed XIAO RP2040. This is my very first project!

BOM:
- RotaryEncoder_Alps_EC11E-Switch_Vertical_H20mm
- XIAO-RP2040-DIP
- Button_Switch_Keyboard:SW_Cherry_MX_1.00u_PCB
- LED_SMD:LED_SK6812MINI_PLCC4_3.5x3.5mm_P1.75mm
